2. #

Define operator "%", A % B means get the remainder after applying A / B
$$
\begin{aligned}

gcd(59400, 16200)

&= gcd(16200, 59400 \% 16200) \\

&= gcd(16200, 10800) \\

&= gcd(10800, 16200 \% 10800)\\

&= gcd(10800, 5400) \\

&= gcd(5400, 10800 \% 5400) \\

&= gcd(5400, 0) \\

&= 5400 \\
\end{aligned}
$$
